# Savolitinib (HMPL-504, AZD6094, ORPATHYS)

Savolitinib is a selective MET tyrosine kinase inhibitor jointly developed for MET-driven cancers, including EGFR-mutant NSCLC with MET amplification after progression on EGFR TKIs such as osimertinib.

## Mechanism

Savolitinib blocks aberrant MET signaling caused by amplification, overexpression, or MET exon 14 skipping alterations. Keywords: oncogene, mutation, receptor, kinase, inhibitor, pathway.

## Combination use

Clinical development includes combinations with osimertinib to address acquired MET-driven resistance.

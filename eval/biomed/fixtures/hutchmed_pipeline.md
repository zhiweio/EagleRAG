# HUTCHMED Our Pipeline (public summary)

Source: https://www.hutch-med.com/pipeline-and-products/our-pipeline/

HUTCHMED is a commercial-stage biopharmaceutical company discovering, developing and commercializing targeted therapies and immunotherapies for cancer and immunological diseases.

## Marketed / late-stage oncology assets (public)

- **Fruquintinib (HMPL-013)** — selective oral VEGFR-1/2/3 inhibitor; metastatic colorectal cancer (mCRC); combinations with PD-1 (e.g. sintilimab) in renal cell carcinoma under study/approval pathways. Brands include ELUNATE (China) and FRUZAQLA (ex-China partner Takeda).
- **Savolitinib (HMPL-504 / AZD6094)** — selective MET TKI; MET-driven NSCLC including combinations with osimertinib; partnered with AstraZeneca (ORPATHYS).
- **Surufatinib (HMPL-012)** — angio-immuno kinase inhibitor (VEGFR / FGFR / CSF-1R); neuroendocrine tumors (NET); combinations explored in PDAC with PD-1 / chemotherapy.

# Fruquintinib (HMPL-013, ELUNATE / FRUZAQLA)

Fruquintinib is a selective oral inhibitor of VEGFR-1, VEGFR-2 and VEGFR-3 used in the treatment of metastatic colorectal cancer (mCRC). It is a key commercial oncology asset in the HUTCHMED pipeline and is studied in combinations with PD-1 inhibitors such as sintilimab in renal cell carcinoma (RCC).

## Mechanism

By blocking VEGFR kinase activity, fruquintinib inhibits tumor angiogenesis and reduces neovascular support for solid tumors. Keywords: kinase, inhibitor, receptor, pathway, angiogenesis, phosphorylation.

## Clinical context

Evidence spans randomized studies in previously treated mCRC and combination regimens with immunotherapy. Competitive landscape includes regorafenib and other anti-angiogenic TKIs.

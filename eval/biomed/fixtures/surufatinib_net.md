# Surufatinib (HMPL-012, sulfatinib, SULANDA)

Surufatinib is an angio-immuno kinase inhibitor targeting VEGFR, FGFR and CSF-1R. It is marketed for neuroendocrine tumors (NET) and explored in combinations (for example with camrelizumab) in pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma (PDAC).

## Mechanism

Dual anti-angiogenic (VEGFR/FGFR) and macrophage-modulating (CSF-1R) activity supports both vascular and immune microenvironment effects. Keywords: immunotherapy, kinase, inhibitor, biomarker, pathway.
